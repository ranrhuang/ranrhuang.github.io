Ranran Huang

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at MatchLab, Imperial College London (2024.4-), under the supervision of Professor Krystian Mikolajczyk. I have broad interests in computer vision and machine learning, and now mainly working on 3D vision.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a research engineer at Meituan and ByteDance. I received my Master’s degree from Tsinghua University, where I was supervised by Professor Wang Yu. During my Master’s studies, I also interned at Xilinx (now AMD) and SenseTime. I earned my Bachelor’s degree from Wuhan University.
